# Google Play listing - English (United States)

## App name

KiNDD - NDD Resource Navigator

## Short description

Find LA County developmental services, regional centers, and next steps.

## Full description

KiNDD - NDD Resource Navigator helps families and clinicians explore
developmental disability resources across Los Angeles County.

Use KiNDD to:

- identify the regional center serving a ZIP code
- browse a map and searchable list of service providers
- filter resources by age group, service, diagnosis, insurance, and distance
- call providers or open directions and websites
- ask KiNDD for AI-generated general information about regional centers,
  assessments, and next steps

Location access is optional. If you allow it, KiNDD uses your location to show
nearby resources. You can also search by ZIP code.

KiNDD is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any
medical condition. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical
advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Provider details can change; confirm
availability, eligibility, services, and coverage directly with each
organization.

KiNDD is an independent community resource and is not affiliated with the
California Department of Developmental Services or any regional center.

Questions or feedback: support@kinddhelp.org
