Find ABA therapy and developmental disability resources across Los Angeles County with KiNDD, your free guide to Regional Center services.

### Notes for Reviewer
```
KiNDD is a free resource finder for families seeking developmental disability services in Los Angeles County, California.

Key features to review:
1. HOME: Tap "Home" tab to see the redesigned homepage with quick actions
2. REGIONAL CENTERS: Tap "Regions" tab to see the map with List/Map toggle (defaults to Map)
3. LANGUAGE: Go to Settings > Language to switch between English and Spanish
4. AI CHAT: Tap the sparkle button to ask questions (responds in selected language)
5. ABOUT/FAQ: Scroll to bottom of Home and tap "About KINDD" or "FAQ"

Location permission is requested to find the user's assigned Regional Center based on their location. The app functions without location access using ZIP code search.

All provider data is publicly available information about ABA therapy providers in LA County.
```
